Wplace AR Viewer

This app displays wplace.live tiles in the sky using
augmented reality.

Required permissions:

📷 Camera - to overlay the tiles over your camera feed
🌍 GPS - to get the right tiles over your location
🧭 Motion sensors - to track your device orientation and look around

The app will still work to an extent without these permissions.

Your data is processed locally on device and not stored anywhere.
(you'll just have to trust me, click on random links on the internet at your own risk)
