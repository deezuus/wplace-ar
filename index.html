Wplace AR Viewer

Augmented reality viewer to display wplace.live artwork in the sky.

Required permissions:

📷 Camera - to overlay the tiles over your camera feed
🌍 GPS - to get the right tiles over your location
🧭 Motion sensors - to track your device orientation and look around

Some features may not work without these permissions.

Your data is processed locally on device and not stored anywhere.
